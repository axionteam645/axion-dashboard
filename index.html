Many token ecosystems collapse when liquidity or structural trust disappears suddenly, leaving users exposed to severe downside.

Uncontrolled supply expansion weakens long-term value and destroys confidence in token-based systems.

Lack of Fee Transparency

Opaque fee logic creates uncertainty, weakens accountability, and makes token behavior harder to evaluate.

Whale Manipulation

DeFi often remains inaccessible because products are built for technical users instead of broader market clarity.
